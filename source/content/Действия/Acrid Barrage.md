**Эффект:** Each enemy in your aura takes @Damage[ternary(gte(@actor.level, 18), 7, ternary(gte(@actor.level, 15), 6, 5))d6[acid]] damage and @Damage[2d6[persistent,acid]] damage with a @Check[reflex|against:class-spell|basic] save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher.
