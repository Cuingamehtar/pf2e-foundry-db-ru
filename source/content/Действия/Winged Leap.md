---
title: Winged Leap
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Частота:** Раз в раунд

---

**Эффект:** Your dragon companion Flies. If it doesn't normally have a fly Speed, it gains a fly Speed of 25 feet for this movement. If it isn't on solid ground at the end of this movement, it falls.
