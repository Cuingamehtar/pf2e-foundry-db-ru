---
title: Bounce Away
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Частота:** Раз в 10 минут

**Триггер:** A melee Strike hits you and deals bludgeoning damage

---

**Эффект:** You gain resistance equal to your level to bludgeoning damage from the triggering Strike, then Stride up to half your Speed. You can Fly instead of Striding if you have a fly Speed.
