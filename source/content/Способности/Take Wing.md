---
title: Take Wing
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Частота:** Раз в раунд

---

You have strengthened your wings and can now fly for short distances. You Fly. If you don't normally have a fly Speed, you gain a fly Speed of 15 feet for this movement. If you aren't on solid ground at the end of this movement, you fall.
