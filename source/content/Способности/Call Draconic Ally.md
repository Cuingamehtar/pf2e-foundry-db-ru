title: Call Draconic Ally
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

Either by beseeching a dragon or bending it to your will, you conjure a mighty creature to assist you. Once per day while you are [[Compendium.pf2e.actionspf2e.Item.Ce9vzE3XWn0u5NcL|Channeling Draconic Essence]], you can cast [[Заклинания/Призыв дракона]] as an innate spell with a tradition matching your draconic exemplar, heightened to half your level rounded up.
