---
title: Call Draconic Ally
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

Either by beseeching a dragon or bending it to your will, you conjure a mighty creature to assist you. Once per day while you are [[Действия/Channel Draconic Essence|Channeling Draconic Essence]], you can cast [[Заклинания/Призыв дракона]] as an innate spell with a tradition matching your draconic exemplar, heightened to half your level rounded up.
