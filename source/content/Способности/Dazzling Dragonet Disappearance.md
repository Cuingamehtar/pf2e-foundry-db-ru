---
title: Dazzling Dragonet Disappearance
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Триггер:** An adjacent enemy hits you with an attack.

---

A brilliant sheen on your scales flashes when you're hit, then you're nowhere to be found. The triggering enemy becomes [[Состояния/Ослеплён]] for 1 round, and you become affected by a 4th-rank [[Заклинания/Невидимость]] spell.
