---
title: Energize Bite
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Требования:** Your most recent action was to use [[Способности/Дыхание дракона [Драконья кровь]|Breath of the Dragon]].

---

The deadly energy of your breath remains in your jaws to empower your attacks. Until the end of your turn, your jaws unarmed attack deals an extra 1d6 damage of a type matching that of your draconic benefactor. This ability has the trait associated with the type of damage it deals.
