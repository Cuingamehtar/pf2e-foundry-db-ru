---
title: Voice of the Elements
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

You inherit a primal dragon's bond to base elements. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Diplomacy checks to [[/act make-an-impression]] on creatures with the elemental trait. You can also ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the Diplomacy skill with elementals that don't speak any language.
