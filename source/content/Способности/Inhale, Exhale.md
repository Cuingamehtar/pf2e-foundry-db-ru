---
title: Inhale, Exhale!
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

You take an especially deep breath as a prelude to unleashing your dragonet breath on your foes. If your next action is to use your [[Способности/Dragonet Breath]], add 5 feet to the length of the cone or line. If your breath deals damage, you also deal one additional die of damage.
