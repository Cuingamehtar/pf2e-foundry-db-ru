---
title: Guided Hover
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

**Триггер:** Ваш ход заканчивается.

**Требования:** Your airborne dragon mount has not used the Fly action this turn.

---

You guide your draconic companion, whose outstretched wings fill with unseen thermals. Your mount hovers without needing to use a Fly action to stay in place.
