---
title: Dragonet Immunities
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

You will not fall asleep unless you choose to allow it, nor will you sit still. Your resistances to sleep and paralysis increase, letting you completely ignore such effects, as befits a dragon. You gain immunity to sleep effects and effects that would make you [[Состояния/Парализован]].
