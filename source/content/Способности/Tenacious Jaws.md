---
title: Tenacious Jaws
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

Your teeth are curved or hooked to hold on to your enemies. Your jaws unarmed attack gains the grapple trait. When you use your [[Способности/Дыхание дракона [Драконья кровь]|Breath of the Dragon]], an opponent [[Состояния/Схвачен]] or [[Состояния/Сдерживаем]] in your jaws uses the outcome one degree of success worse than the result of their saving throw.
