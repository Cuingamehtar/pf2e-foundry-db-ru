---
title: Benefactor's Strike
---
**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

Your benefactor's blood flows through your veins, and you channel their power into your body. When you critically hit with an unarmed Strike, the target takes an additional 1 persistent damage of the same type as the damage dealt by the breath of your draconic benefactor.
