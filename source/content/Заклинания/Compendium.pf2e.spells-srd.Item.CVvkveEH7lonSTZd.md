<p>You channel your heightened sense of self-worth into a creature, forcing them to acknowledge your true worth. You can Cast this Spell using 1 action if you know the target isn't worthy of you spending your time to generate the full force of this spell, or using 3 actions if you can't possibly hold back the majesty of your presence and must flood the target with your full attention.</p><p>The target takes 5d10 mental damage and other effects depending on its Will saving throw. If you Cast the Spell using 1 action, the target gets a result one degree of success better than it rolled.</p><hr /><p><strong>Критический успех:</strong> Существо невредимо.</p><p><strong>Успех:</strong> The target takes half damage and is [[Compendium.pf2e.conditionitems.Item.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]] with you for 1 round.</p><p><strong>Провал:</strong> The target takes full damage and is fascinated with you for 1 round. While fascinated with you, the target must succeed at a @Check[flat|dc:5] when attempting to Cast a Spell or use an action with the auditory trait. On a failure, the action is disrupted as the target blurts praise for you instead of saying what it intended.</p><p><strong>Критический провал:</strong> As failure, but double damage and the effect lasts for 1 minute.</p><hr /><p><strong>Усиление (+1):</strong> Урон увеличивается на 1d10.</p>
