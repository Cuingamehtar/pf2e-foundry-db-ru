**Источник:** Pathfinder Lost Omens Draconic Codex

- - -

<p><strong>Область:</strong> (continued) two 30-foot cones</p><hr /><p>Like a vorpal dragon, you absorb life with an inhale and release it with an exhale. All living creatures in the first cone take 5d6 void damage with a basic Fortitude save. You and all living creatures in the second cone regain Hit Points equal to the greatest damage a single creature took from the first cone.</p><hr /><p><strong>Усиление (+1):</strong> The damage and healing increase by 1d6.</p>
